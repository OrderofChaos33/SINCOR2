CUSTOMER FLOW ($99 AUTO DETAILING MEDIA PACK):
================================================================================

1. LANDING PAGE (templates/auto_detailing_mediapack.html)
   Customer fills form with business info
   ↓

2. PAYPAL CHECKOUT (Integrated in HTML)
   - Uses PayPal SDK with your credentials
   - Customer pays $99
   - Returns order_id + payer_id
   ↓

3. BACKEND API (/api/auto-detailing/generate-mediapack)
   POST with form data + PayPal order_id
   ↓

4. CONTENT GENERATION PIPELINE:

   A. LEAD INGEST (services/lead_ingest/app.py)
      - Captures customer data
      - Validates payment
      - Creates lead record
      ↓

   B. LEAD ROUTER (services/lead_router/app.py)
      - Routes to outreach_mediapax generator
      - Assigns to content_pipeline
      ↓

   C. MEDIAPAX GENERATOR (services/outreach_mediapax/generator.py)
      - generate_pack(lead_data)
      - Creates 8+ marketing materials:
        * Flyer (print + digital)
        * Facebook/Instagram ads (5 variations)
        * Google My Business posts (10)
        * Email newsletter
        * Before/After photo frames
        * Price list
        * Business card
      ↓

   D. CONTENT PERSONALIZATION (content_personalization_engine.py)
      - Customizes with business name, phone, email
      - Applies branding
      - Generates variations
      ↓

   E. CONTENT QUALITY (content_quality_engine.py)
      - Quality checks all materials
      - Ensures 90%+ quality score
      - Validates outputs
      ↓

5. EMAIL DELIVERY (email_automation.py)
   - send_mediapack_email(customer_email, files)
   - Attaches all generated materials
   - Includes download links
   - Sends confirmation
   ↓

6. CUSTOMER RECEIVES:
   - Email with all 8+ materials
   - Instant download links
   - Setup instructions
   - Support contact
