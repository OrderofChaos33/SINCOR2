IS IT FAIR? Fairness Checklist
================================================================================

✅ Equal start:         All agents begin at 3% commission
✅ Transparent pricing: All marketplace items listed with prices
✅ Meritocratic:        Better agents can upgrade faster (they earn more)
✅ No gatekeeping:      All tiers available to all agents
✅ Visible ROI:         Agents can calculate payback (1-4 deals)
✅ No predatory lock-in: Can withdraw anytime (minimal fees)
✅ Team fair:           Sub-agents earn 100% of own commissions + you get %
✅ Equity fair:         Only available after $5,000+ earned (prevents scammers)
✅ Compound benefits:   Early wins accelerate exponentially
✅ Transparent metrics: Can see exactly what peers earned + what tiers they bought

Most agent-based systems fail because:
  - Flat commission (no upside) → burnout

  - Team "benefits" that are really MLM → anger
  - Hidden pricing → confusion

SINCOR avoids all of these.
