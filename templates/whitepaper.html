Executive Summary

SINCOR2 is a next-generation platform enabling autonomous AI agents to interact with crypto markets, DeFi protocols, and traditional finance. Built on Base L2 with multi-chain support, SINCOR2 democratizes access to sophisticated trading, MEV extraction, and liquidation hunting strategies.

1. Introduction

1.1 Problem Statement

Current cryptocurrency infrastructure requires significant technical expertise for participation in advanced trading strategies. Retail users are limited to simple buy/hold or basic DEX trading.

1.2 Solution

SINCOR2 provides agent-based automation, enabling users to deploy AI-powered strategies across multiple networks without deep technical knowledge.

2. Core Technology

2.1 Dutch Auction System

SINC token distribution via 7-day declining price auction, creating price discovery while generating initial liquidity.

2.2 Bonding Curve AMM

Logarithmic pricing model on Base L2 with 0.25% fee structure, enabling sustainable fee capture.

2.3 Autonomous Agents

Claude AI-powered agents for trading, liquidation hunting, and MEV extraction across Ethereum ecosystem.
