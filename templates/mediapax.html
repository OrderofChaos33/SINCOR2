MediaPax FAQ

Common questions about our media pack service

What information do you need from me to create my MediaPax?

We gather most information automatically from your website, Google My Business, and public sources. You'll receive a brief questionnaire covering any gaps and specific preferences for positioning and messaging.

How do you ensure my MediaPax stands out from competitors?

We research your competitive landscape and identify unique differentiators, customer pain points, and positioning opportunities. Each MediaPax emphasizes what makes your business unique and valuable.

Can I see examples of MediaPax presentations before ordering?

Due to client confidentiality, we can't share actual MediaPax presentations. However, we can provide industry-specific mockups and discuss how your presentation would be structured during a consultation call.

What if I need revisions to my MediaPax?

Each package includes one round of revisions based on your feedback. Additional revisions can be made for $50 each. We work with you to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

How do you target and present MediaPax to prospects?

We identify ideal prospects in your service area using demographic data, business triggers, and behavioral indicators. The MediaPax is then presented through personalized email campaigns, social media outreach, and direct contact methods.

Do you provide the prospect list or handle outreach yourself?

We handle the complete process - from prospect identification to MediaPax presentation. You receive qualified leads and interested prospects ready for your sales process. No prospect lists or manual outreach required.

Can I use my MediaPax for my own marketing efforts?

Absolutely! Your MediaPax becomes your property and can be used for website content, sales presentations, trade shows, email marketing, and any other business development activities.

What's the typical response rate from MediaPax presentations?

Response rates vary by industry and market, but typically range from 8-15% for initial interest, with 20-35% of those converting to qualified leads. This is significantly higher than traditional cold outreach methods.
