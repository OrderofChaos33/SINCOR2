FAQ

Enterprise Questions

Who is Enterprise designed for?
Companies doing $1M+ in revenue who want to scale without proportional headcount growth. Agencies who want to white-label and resell. Enterprises with complex tech stacks that need deep integration. Organizations in regulated industries that need compliance controls.
What does "white-label" actually mean?
You get the full SINCOR platform running under your brand — your logo, your domain, your color scheme. Your clients see your product, not SINCOR. You can charge whatever you want for it. Many Enterprise customers resell it at 3–5x their subscription cost.
How does custom agent development work?
You describe what you need — the workflow, the inputs, the expected outputs. Our team scopes it, builds it, deploys it alongside your existing agents, and maintains it. Up to 5 custom agents are included in Enterprise. Additional custom agents can be added at an agreed fee.
What is SBT integration?
SBT (System-By-Target) integration means our agents connect directly to your existing business systems — your CRM, ERP, databases, or proprietary APIs. The agents work inside your data ecosystem rather than requiring you to migrate to ours.
Is there a contract or minimum term?
No contracts. Month-to-month. You can cancel anytime. That said, most Enterprise customers see ROI in month 1 and have never looked back.
