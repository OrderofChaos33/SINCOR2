Why a bonding curve instead of a DEX pool?

Bonding curves give a deterministic price floor (math, not market). Buyers know the exact price function. LP gets burned at graduation, which is mechanically impossible to reverse. The owner stake is on a 24-month Sablier vest. The market structure is designed to be honest from day one.
