We retain your data for as long as your account is active, plus 90 days after cancellation. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, email court@getsincor.com

7. GDPR (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR. Our legal bases for processing are contract performance, legitimate interests, and legal obligation. You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of sale (we do not sell data). Contact us at court@getsincor.com to exercise these rights.

9. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for data in transit and at rest. We conduct regular security audits. Despite our efforts, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Children

SINCOR is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
