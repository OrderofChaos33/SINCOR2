1. Who We Are

SINCOR ("SINCOR," "we," "us," or "our") operates the AI business automation platform at getsincor.com. We are the data controller for personal data collected through the platform. For privacy-related questions, contact privacy@getsincor.com.

2. Information We Collect

Category Examples Purpose

Payment Information Billing address; payment transactions processed via PayPal (we do not store full card numbers) Process payments; prevent fraud
Business Data Target customer profiles, CRM data, brand guidelines, content assets you upload Deliver the AI automation services you've purchased
Communications Messages you send via support tickets, email, or in-platform chat Provide support; improve services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Services – run AI agents, generate deliverables, maintain platform functionality
  • Process transactions – handle payments, issue invoices, manage subscriptions
  • Communicate with you – send service updates, receipts, security alerts, and (with your consent) marketing emails
  • Provide customer support – respond to your requests and troubleshoot issues
  • Security and fraud prevention – detect and prevent unauthorized access and abuse
  • Legal compliance – comply with applicable laws, respond to legal process, enforce our terms
  • Improve our platform – analyze anonymized, aggregated usage data to make the platform better

We do not use your data to train publicly available AI models or sell your data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance – to deliver the Services you've purchased
  • Legitimate interests – security, fraud prevention, service improvement (where not overridden by your rights)
  • Consent – for marketing communications and non-essential cookies (you can withdraw consent at any time)
  • Legal obligation – where required by applicable law

5. Sharing Your Information

We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers – trusted vendors who assist with cloud hosting (AWS/GCP), payment processing (PayPal), email delivery, analytics, and customer support, under strict data processing agreements
  • Business transfers – if SINCOR is acquired or merges with another company, your data may transfer to the new owner, who will be bound by this policy
  • Legal requirements – when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of SINCOR, our users, or the public
  • With your consent – for any other purpose with your explicit consent

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third-party marketers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. After account termination:

  • Account data and deliverables: deleted within 90 days (you may export within the first 30 days)
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years for financial and tax compliance
  • Security logs: retained for 12 months
  • Backup copies: deleted within 30 days after primary deletion

7. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies – required for the platform to function (cannot be disabled)
  • Preference cookies – remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand how users interact with the platform (can be disabled)
  • Marketing cookies – used only with your consent to personalize content (can be disabled)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access – request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction – request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion – request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability – receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection – object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Restriction – request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw Consent – withdraw consent for consent-based processing at any time

To exercise any right, email privacy@getsincor.com. We will respond within 30 days. EEA/UK users may lodge a complaint with their national data protection authority.

9. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Role-based access controls limiting who can access your data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Immutable audit logs for all data access
  • Intrusion detection and 24/7 monitoring
  • Incident response procedures with 72-hour breach notification

For full details, see our Security & Compliance page.

10. International Data Transfers

SINCOR operates primarily in the United States. If you are located outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. For EEA/UK users, we ensure adequate protection through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Delete personal information (with exceptions for legal obligations)
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@getsincor.com or call our privacy line.

12. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at privacy@getsincor.com.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email and/or a prominent notice on our website at least 30 days before changes take effect. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

14. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests:

  • Email: privacy@getsincor.com
  • Response time: Within 30 days for rights requests; within 72 hours for breach notifications
  • Website: getsincor.com
