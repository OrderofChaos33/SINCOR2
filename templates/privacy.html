1. Introduction

SINCOR ("we," "our," or "us") operates getsincor.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our AI-powered business automation services.

By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Name and email address (waitlist signups, contact forms)
  • Company name and business information
  • Phone number (enterprise inquiries)
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers)
  • Communications with us (support tickets, emails)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Service
  • Referring website addresses
  • Clickstream data and interaction patterns

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance user experience and collect usage data. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Communications: Send updates, marketing materials, and respond to inquiries
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns and optimize performance
  • Security: Detect, prevent, and address fraud or technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Business Operations: Process transactions and manage accounts

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting in operations (email delivery, payment processing, analytics)

  • With Consent: When you explicitly authorize disclosure

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service may integrate with third-party services:

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain business records

When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize data.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

8.2 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have:

  • Right to be informed about data processing
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

8.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@getsincor.com

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

We implement safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers.
