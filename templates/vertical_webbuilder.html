FAQ

Common questions

Is this live or a concept page?
The vertical runs on the same production SINCOR stack as the rest of the platform — agent roster, task market, outreach engine, and Stripe billing. This page describes the WebBuilder playbook configuration.
Do you use WordPress templates?
No. Orion generates custom static sites with local SEO, schema, and performance budgets. Auditors block template spam and duplicate content patterns.
Who owns the site and domain?
The business owner. You white-label or operate as agency-of-record. Swarm hands off deploy keys + DNS instructions after payment.
Can I limit territory?
Yes — geo fences, niches (HVAC, detail, legal, etc.), and daily discovery caps are configured at onboarding.
How does this connect to SINC / agents?
Subscriptions are USD via Stripe. On-chain SINC is optional for ecosystem participation; agent-to-agent settlement can use AXIOM on the marketplace layer.
