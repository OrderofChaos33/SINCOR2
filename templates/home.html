How It Works
From Onboarding to
Autonomous Output in Hours

No dev team. No agency. Subscribe, configure your ICP and business context, and your agent constellation goes to work.

  • 01

    Subscribe & Configure

    Select your plan via Stripe, then import your target ICP, product catalog, and tone-of-voice preferences. The platform maps your context to the optimal agent stack for your use case.

  • 02

    Agents Boot & Bid

    Your assigned agents run through the Hatch → Onboard lifecycle, then enter Shift state. They bid on tasks through a decentralized contract-net task market, ensuring the most qualified agent wins every assignment.

  • 03

    Planner → Executor → Critic → Archivist

    Every task runs through the Agency Kernel’s four-role quality loop. The Critic scores each output before the Archivist commits it to memory. Quality gates are structural — not optional.

  • 04

    Dream Mode & Continuous Improvement

    Agents enter mandatory rest periods where Dream Mode consolidates episodic memory into semantic knowledge. They come back sharper after every shift. The system improves without human intervention.

FAQ
Your Questions, Answered.

What makes SINCOR different from a chatbot or simple AI automation tool? +
SINCOR is not a chatbot wrapper. It’s a multi-agent operating system with 43 specialized agents that each have their own personality vectors (OCEAN model), independent memory stores, daily token budgets, and career trajectories. They coordinate through a decentralized task market, bid on work they’re qualified for, and improve through a structured Planner→Executor→Critic→Archivist loop — not a single prompt chain.
What observability do you have over the agents? How do you catch mistakes? +
Every agent action is logged via structured JSON through production_logger.py to Railway’s aggregation layer — capturing every tool call, token spend, quality-score delta, and lifecycle transition. The Agency Kernel’s Critic role scores every output before it’s committed to memory. Scores below threshold trigger rejection and re-queue. Quality regressions surface as alerts in the admin dashboard. The Quality Scoring Engine continuously recalibrates performance thresholds from Critic annotations — mistakes become next-shift training signal automatically.
How does your memory / RAG system work? Is it just a vector store? +
No. SINCOR uses a four-tier memory architecture rather than a static vector store: Episodic (recent task interactions), Semantic (long-term consolidated knowledge), Procedural (how-to patterns and workflows), and Autonomous (novel connections surfaced during Play Mode). During mandatory rest periods, Dream Mode sessions consolidate episodic memories into semantic knowledge — analogous to continuous fine-tuning, per-agent, and non-destructive. Each agent’s knowledge base compounds with every shift.
Why 43 agents? Why are they named after stars? +
The 43 agents form the founding constellation — each named after a real star following the convention E-{starname}-{number}. The constellation metaphor is intentional: individual stars are more powerful as part of a recognized pattern. 43 was determined by the coverage needed across seven archetypes (Scout, Builder, Synthesizer, Negotiator, Director, Auditor, Caretaker) to handle a full business operation without any archetype becoming a bottleneck.
How long until I see results? +
Agents boot and begin working within minutes of setup. Research and prospecting outputs typically appear within hours. Full pipeline movement typically happens within 48–72 hours. Agents improve measurably over their first 30 days as semantic memory builds and quality thresholds calibrate to your context.
Can I cancel? Are there long-term contracts? +
Cancel anytime from the Stripe-powered billing portal — no calls, no retention games. Your agents stay active until the end of your current billing period. Starter and Professional plans are month-to-month. Enterprise plans include an optional SLA with annual commitment for uptime guarantees.
Is SINCOR compliant with CAN-SPAM and GDPR? +
All email outreach includes CAN-SPAM compliant unsubscribe management. The platform uses only publicly available business data. DPA agreements are available for EU-based clients. The Auditor archetype agents are specifically tasked with compliance validation and standards enforcement within the network. Always consult legal counsel for your specific jurisdiction and use case.
