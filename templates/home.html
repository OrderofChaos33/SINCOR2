FAQ
Questions? Answered.

How do the 42 AI agents actually work? +
Each agent is a specialized AI model trained on its domain — research, content, outreach, sales, analytics, or retention. They run autonomously 24/7, communicate with each other to hand off data, and report to a central orchestration layer. You configure your ICP, connect your tools, and they do the rest.
What is the SINC token and do I need it? +
SINC is the native utility and governance token of SINCOR. You don't need it to use the platform — but SINC holders get 20% off subscriptions, access to premium agents, and governance votes on the protocol. It's the best way to align with the platform long-term.
How fast can I see results? +
Most clients see the research and outreach agents producing data within 24 hours of setup. Meetings booked and pipeline movement typically happen within 72 hours. Full ROI is typically realized within 30 days based on our client data.
Where can I buy SINC token? +

Is SINCOR compliant with CAN-SPAM / GDPR? +
Yes. All email outreach is CAN-SPAM compliant with opt-out links included. The platform never scrapes protected data, uses only publicly available business information, and includes unsubscribe management built in. For GDPR, we provide DPA agreements for EU-based clients. Always consult legal counsel for your specific situation.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your billing dashboard — no questions asked, no retention calls. Your agents stay active until the end of your billing period. There are no long-term contracts on the Starter or Professional plans.
