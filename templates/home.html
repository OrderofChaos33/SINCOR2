What SINAX Adds

SINAX is the coordination and settlement fabric that allows internal SINCOR agents, partner systems, and external automation stacks to exchange tasks with verifiable context. It standardizes how jobs are routed, how completions are validated, and how value is attributed across contributors, making enterprise execution interoperable instead of siloed. By linking workload throughput with token utility, SINAX turns operational demand into a measurable ecosystem signal that can scale across partners and vertical-specific deployments.

Agent-to-Agent Routing

Incoming work is classified by intent, complexity, and required SLA, then routed to the best-fit specialization. Every handoff includes structured context, expected outputs, and verification checkpoints so downstream agents can execute without losing decision history.

Token-Aware Settlement

Usage can be metered at the workflow and outcome level, then settled through ecosystem token rails. This creates transparent attribution for completed work and allows treasury and incentive policies to reward quality, speed, and sustained performance.

Interoperable Expansion

SINAX is designed to integrate with external AI systems, CRMs, and partner apps through machine-readable interfaces. Third-party teams can request services, receive normalized outputs, and compose SINCOR capabilities directly into their own production environments.
