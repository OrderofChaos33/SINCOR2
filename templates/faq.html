Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services

General Questions

What is SINCOR and what do you do?

SINCOR is a comprehensive business intelligence and lead generation platform. We build professional media packs for businesses, deploy AI-powered lead generation systems, and provide advanced business intelligence tools to help companies grow their revenue automatically.

How is SINCOR different from other lead generation companies?

Unlike traditional lead gen companies that wait for customers to find you, we proactively research businesses, create professional media packs using their information, and present these packs to prospects who didn't even know they needed those services. We don't cold call - we dangle opportunities in front of decision-makers.

What industries do you work with?

We specialize in service-based businesses including auto detailing, pest control, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, roofing, and many other trades. Our system works for any business that needs consistent lead flow.

How quickly can I expect to see results?

Media packs are typically built and deployed within 3-5 business days. Lead generation campaigns begin showing results within the first week, with full momentum typically achieved within 2-3 weeks of campaign launch.

Do you guarantee results?

While we cannot guarantee specific numbers due to market variables, our media pack and lead generation systems have a proven track record. We provide detailed analytics and continuously optimize campaigns based on performance data.

What's included in your pricing?

All pricing includes setup, deployment, monitoring, and basic optimization. Premium plans include advanced analytics, multiple campaign types, and dedicated account management. No hidden fees or surprise charges.

Can I cancel anytime?

Media packs are one-time purchases. Subscription services like Hunter and Cor-Tec's AI can be cancelled with 30 days notice. All data and assets created remain yours even after cancellation.

Do you work with competitors in the same area?

We maintain territorial exclusivity for direct competitors in the same service area. However, we may work with businesses in different markets or complementary service categories within the same geographic region.

MediaPax - Professional Media Packs

What exactly is a media pack?

A media pack is a professional presentation that showcases a business's services, capabilities, and value proposition. We research your business information, create compelling content, and format it into a presentation that gets shown to prospects as if it were created specifically for them.

How do you gather my business information?

We use automated research tools to gather publicly available information about your business, services, reviews, and market positioning. We then organize this into professional templates that highlight your unique value propositions.

What's the difference between Basic ($175), Premium ($275), and Enterprise ($400) media packs?

Premium ($275): Everything in Basic plus incentive strategies, follow-up sequences, upsell campaigns
Enterprise ($400): Everything in Premium plus lead generation plan setup, competitive analysis, ongoing optimization

How many leads can I expect with Hunter?

Lead volume varies by market, competition, and service type. Typically, businesses see 15-50 qualified leads per month in the first tier, with higher tiers delivering proportionally more leads plus better qualification and faster follow-up.

What's included in the Hunter service?

Unlimited prospect discovery, automated outreach campaigns, lead qualification and scoring, multi-channel follow-up sequences, performance analytics, and monthly strategy optimization.
