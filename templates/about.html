Our Story

How SINCOR became the leading business intelligence platform

2019

Founded with a Vision

SINCOR was founded by a team of data scientists and business analysts who recognized that traditional BI tools were too complex and expensive for most businesses. We set out to create a platform that would make advanced analytics accessible to everyone.

2020

First Product Launch

We launched our initial platform with core analytics features and attracted our first 1,000 users within six months. The response was overwhelming, validating our belief that businesses were hungry for better analytics tools.

2021

AI Integration

We integrated advanced AI and machine learning capabilities, introducing predictive analytics and automated insights. This positioned SINCOR as a leader in next-generation business intelligence.

2024

Enterprise Excellence

Today, SINCOR serves over 10,000 active users and 500+ enterprise clients worldwide. Our platform processes billions of data points daily and continues to evolve with cutting-edge features and capabilities.
