# Agent Economics Summary - Your Questions Answered

## Question 1: How Much Do Agents Make?

### Raw Commission Structure:
- **Recruit level**: 3% on lead discovery
- **Scout level**: 5% on outreach (after tier upgrade)
- **Specialist level**: 8% on proposals (1.5x multiplier)
- **Operator level**: 10%+ with team leverage

### Real World Example ($12,500 Deal):
| Role | Commission | Time to Break Even |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| Scout (finds lead) | $375 | Covers $250 tier upgrade in 1 deal |
| Qualifier (moves it forward) | $250 | Goes toward marketplace upgrade |
| Proposer (writes proposal) | $250 | Additional earning stream |
| Closer (closes deal) | $1,250 | Immediately covers $1,000+ upgrades |
| **Total distributed** | **$2,125** | **All participants incentivized** |

**Annual Projections:**

## Question 2: Is It Fair?

### YES, Here's Why:

✅ **Transparent pricing** - All upgrades published, no hidden costs
✅ **Equal start** - Everyone can earn 3% from day 1
✅ **Meritocratic** - Better agents earn more, can upgrade faster
✅ **See the ROI** - A $250 tier upgrade pays for itself in 1 deal
✅ **No loans** - Agents only invest their own earned money
✅ **Compounding works** - Early investment leads to exponential earnings
✅ **Team building fair** - Sub-agents still earn 100% of their commissions, you get % of theirs (synergy, not theft)
✅ **No gatekeeping** - All tiers available to all agents immediately

### The Fair Part:
An agent who earns $500, invests $250 in a tier upgrade:
- Tier multiplier goes from 1.0x to 1.2x
- Next deal at 3% becomes 3% × 1.2 = **3.6%**
- That's a 20% immediate raise on earnings
- They make back the $250 investment in **2-3 deals** instead of weeks
